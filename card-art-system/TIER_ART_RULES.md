# Tier Art Rules

These rules are for AI Art Card Lab experiments only.

## Emerald

- Green glow, early-premium feel.
- Cleanest and calmest of the premium tiers.
- Avoid overpowered cosmic effects.

## Sapphire

- Blue light, cool contrast, sharper arena reflections.
- Should feel more polished than Emerald without becoming too busy.

## Ruby

- Red energy, heat, stronger action.
- Keep skin tones natural and avoid red wash over the player.

## Amethyst

- Purple/violet lighting with a more rare-card finish.
- Can use richer atmosphere and stronger rim lighting.

## Galaxy

- Highest spectacle tier.
- Cosmic, prismatic, or starfield accents are allowed.
- Must still keep player face, body, OVR zone, and name zone readable.
