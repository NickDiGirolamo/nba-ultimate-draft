# AI Card Style Guide

Use this guide for the isolated AI Art Cards lab only. It should not override the approved live player-card templates.

For durable rules, constraints, and future editing guardrails, start with `AI_CARD_DESIGN_SYSTEM.md`.

## Direction

- Premium full-art basketball card with the player as the first read.
- Clear face, clean jersey silhouette, and readable pose at small card sizes.
- The overall rating always lives in the top-left corner of the card face.
- Natural positions always sit directly beneath the overall rating.
- The player's full first and last name must appear on the card face without abbreviation, clipping, or ellipses.
- Background energy should support the tier, not compete with the player name, OVR, or natural positions.
- Avoid clutter around the top-left rating/position stack and bottom-name zones.

## Readability Checks

- Full player name must remain readable in compact previews.
- Overall number and natural positions must stay clear against the artwork.
- Tier color should be obvious in the frame, lighting, or background accents.
- The generated image should still work in a dense mobile card grid.

## Consistency

- Keep one visual language across tiers: same realism level, lighting quality, and camera treatment.
- Tier identity should change through color, energy, materials, and atmosphere rather than completely different art styles.
