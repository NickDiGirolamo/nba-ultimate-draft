# AI Card Prompt Template

Use this as a starting prompt for generated full-art player card experiments.

```text
Create a premium full-art basketball trading card image of [PLAYER NAME].

Pose:
[POSE OR ACTION]

Tier:
[Emerald | Sapphire | Ruby | Amethyst | Galaxy]

Visual direction:
- realistic premium sports-card artwork
- likeness based on a clear real reference image when this is a real player
- clear face and jersey silhouette
- accurate real-life team uniform: correct base color, trim colors, team wordmark, and jersey number
- dynamic arena lighting
- tier-colored energy accents
- clean space in the top-left for overall rating
- clean space directly beneath the overall rating for natural positions
- clean space on the card face for the player's full first and last name
- no fake typography, no fake logos, no watermark
- vertical card composition

Style consistency:
- sharp subject focus
- polished trading-card finish
- high contrast without noisy background clutter near the top-left rating/position stack or name zone
- readable at small mobile card size
```

## Negative Prompt Ideas

```text
blurry face, wrong player likeness, generic face, incorrect hairstyle, extra fingers, incorrect hands, unreadable jersey, wrong team uniform, wrong jersey number, messy wordmark, fake logos, distorted basketball, over-cluttered background, cropped head, cropped feet, low-resolution, watermark
```
